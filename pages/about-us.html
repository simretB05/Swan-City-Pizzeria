about us

From Small Pizza Restaurant to Community Spot

Swan City Pizzeria started as a small pizza restaurant in the heart of the Grande Prairie. It was founded by two friends, John douod and Mark Albert, who shared a passion for making delicious pizza. Over the years, Swan City Pizzeria became a community spot where people from all walks of life came to enjoy good food and company.John and Mark organized food drives, sponsored community events, and donated to local charities. They also started offering pizza making classes, which helped many people launch their own successful pizza restaurants. Swan City Pizzeria offers job opportunities for people of all ages. earn some money.

Swan city pizzeria also hires older individuals as delivery drivers and kitchen staff, offering them flexible hours and a chance to be part of a fun and supportive team. Swan City Pizzeria believes in promoting from within, so they offer training and development opportunities for all employees. They encourage their staff to learn new skills and take on new challenges, and they reward hard work and dedication with promotions and raises. Today, Swan City Pizzeria remains a beloved community spot that serves delicious pizza and provides job opportunities for people of all ages. They hire teenagers as dishwashers, servers, and cashiers, giving them their first work experience.
