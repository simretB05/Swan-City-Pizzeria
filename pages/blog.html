Pizza and wine make a perfect match. The savory pizza flavors complement the rich and complex tastes of wine. Red wines like Chianti or Cabernet Sauvignon pair well with meaty or tomato-based pizzas, and white wines like Pinot Grigio or Sauvignon Blanc are ideal for lighter pizzas with toppings like mushrooms or spinach.
